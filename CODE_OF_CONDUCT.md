# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Imposia welcoming and harassment-free for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of
technical expertise.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific when discussing code or design.
- Assume good intent while remaining accountable for impact.
- Accept feedback gracefully and keep disagreements focused on the work.
- Protect private information and follow the security reporting policy.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

For conduct incidents, use the repository's private vulnerability-reporting
route described in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) and clearly label the report as
a code-of-conduct matter. If that route is unavailable, request private contact
through a public issue without including sensitive details.

Maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, restrict participation, or ban
contributors when necessary to protect the community. Reports will be handled
as confidentially as practical, with conflicts of interest disclosed and
recused.

This policy is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
